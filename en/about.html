About us

The P.A.S.T. project

P.A.S.T. — Heritage Art History Territory — is a cultural enhancement project born from the collaboration between the Diocese of Ragusa, the Municipality of Ragusa and the Novum 1693 Cultural Workshop Association.

The goal is to make the artistic, architectural and religious heritage of the Ragusa area accessible and alive, connecting the places of faith and history in a single coordinated cultural itinerary.

Through events, special openings, guided tours and the Vitruvio Card, P.A.S.T. aims to bring residents and visitors closer to the beauty of Ragusa, a UNESCO World Heritage city.
